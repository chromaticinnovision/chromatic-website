Founder’s Message 创办人想说的话
“Marketing is not effective simply because it is being done. It only works when it is done in the right direction.”

营销不是“有做就会有效”，而是你有没有做对方向。

Before founding Chromatic, Nixon spent years in the trenches of digital advertising and SEO — learning, campaign by campaign, what actually moves the needle for a business versus what just looks good on a report. That hands-on experience is why Chromatic leads with strategy first: every piece of content, every ad, every platform decision has to serve a clear business direction, not just fill a content calendar.

在创立 Chromatic 之前，Nixon 在数字广告与 SEO 领域累积了多年的实战经验，一场又一场的投放，让他清楚分辨出哪些做法真正能为生意带来成果，哪些只是“看起来漂亮”的报告数字。这也是为什么 Chromatic 始终坚持策略先行——每一份内容、每一个广告、每一个平台决策，都必须服务于清晰的business方向，而不只是把内容日历填满。

— Nixon Law
Founder, Chromatic Innovision Advertising
